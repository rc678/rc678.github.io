About

Hi, I'm Rosheen Chaudhry! I am currently a software developer, Co-op at Colgate-Palmolive. At Colgate, I have completed many projects that involve speeding up and optimizing their existing systems. You can see more details about the projects on my resume


I will graduate Rutgers University, Douglass Residential College in May 2016 with a BS in Computer Science. Douglass Residential College is the only "Women's Residential College" in the US which means that it is the only women's college within a larger University.


I am also heavily involved with the women in technology community at Rutgers University. I am a member of the Douglass-Dimacs Computing Corps in which I help expose and encourage middle school girls and girl scouts about the tech field. I do this through teaching CS in an after school club and holding workshops for the girl scouts. I am also a lead events organizer for HackHers, the Rutger's women's hackathon. I assisted in planning and budgeting for events during the hackathon with the help of a small team of people. In addition, I contacted company and academic representatives to give talks during the hackathon


Furthermore, I have taught many technical workshops to the students at Rutgers. I have given talks about HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Git, and much more . Overall, I am very passionate about programming, teamwork, and promoting gender diversity in the technology field.
